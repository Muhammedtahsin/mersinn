kız kalesi

The historical Maiden's Castle, formerly known as Korykos and also known as the Sea Castle, is one of the biggest tourism centers among the places to visit in Mersin. The castle, which was founded by Leon I on an islet 600 meters from the shore on the coast of the neighborhood of the same name, has been used by many civilizations and reign settlements. He says that the construction of the castle dates back to 1199.

cannet ve cehennem

The Heaven and Hell Potholes, located in Silifke district, with a length of 25 km, came into existence after the lime layers of their places were melted for many years and the water inside was emptied and the carved ceiling structure collapsed. There is also a small church and a temple in the formations, which are divided into two as Heaven's Pitcher and Hell's Pitcher, 80 meters between them. We strongly recommend that you see the potholes included in the museum during your trip to Mersin.

Natural beauties of Mersin

Mersin, which is one of the cities that attract the most tourists in the Mediterranean region, has beautiful natural beauties in its lush geography. Visitors from all over the world marvel at the uniquely beautiful places they visit. Some of Mersin's most visited natural beauties are; Narlıkuyu, Yapraklı Bay, Çamlıyayla Baştepe, Gelincik Hill, Yerköprü Waterfall, Tarsus Waterfall, Çocakdere National Park, Lamos Canyon and Boğsak Bay.
